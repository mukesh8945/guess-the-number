How to play

Guess a Random Number between 1 - 100

Instructions

  1. Guess & enter any random number between 1 - 100.
  2. A message will prompt whether your number is low or high.
  3. If it is low, enter a greater number than before.
  4. If it is high, enter a smaller number than before.
  5. Keep playing until you win/lose, then start a new game.
